Robotics and Hobby-Electronics Stores in India

    This is a list of Best to Worst stores which sell robotics and electronics spares and kits online in India. The factors considered are Cost, Delivery timelines, Accuracy of Product Description, Punctuality of owners, Trust factor and Customer support. I have ordered from majority of these stores in the last 12 years.
    Robotics & Electronics Spares
    • Robu - This is the "close the eyes and just order" store for my hobby projects components.
    • Rhydolabz - 2nd option, but higher delivery charges.
    • BM Embedded solutions - Closed! - Reasonable prices with good support on products. One of my HC-12 module was not working, so I sent a video of it on whatsapp to the store owner. He promptly guided me to solve the issue.
    • Robokits - They manufacture/stock good quality li-ion batteries. Products are little bit costly with higher delivery charges.
    • Robodo - One of my favourite store('Bombay Electronics') in lamnington road operated by a very good and humble Sardarji Uncle. He is Honest, and his store has been my fav destination for DC motors. This was also listed on ebay back then and I had ordered a lots of servo motors, ultrasonic sensors for one my hobby projects.
    • Rare components - Nope! No rare components! Good support, but slow delivery timelines. My order took 12 days to reach from Jaipur to Mumbai.
    • Robotics DNA - Late delivery of products. Prices are okay.
    • Probotronix - Closed! - Low quality products. One of my AMS1117 voltage regulator board burnt, and it hurt my finger. I demamded refund and I got it. So, good customer support and prices are cheapest of all. But, bad quality products.
    • Tomson Electronics - Products are reasonable priced. Good customer support.
    • Roborium - The founder(s) are true robotics enthusiasts. They have some rare collection of robotics kits which are sold internationally.
    • Eagle Electronics - I loved the website user experience. But at the end what matters is the price and delivery timelines. Humble store with but unique range of products in a few categories.
